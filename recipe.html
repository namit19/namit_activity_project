Delicious Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-pupose flour
  • 2 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoon melted butter

Instructions

  1. In a bowl mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt.
  2. In another bowl beat milk and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry items and mix until smooth.
  4. Heat and pour in batter to form pancakes.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until goled.
Enjoy your homemade pancakes!!
